Xiang An (Chinese: 安翔) is a Research Scientist and Team Lead of the Multimodal Large Model Group at GlintLab. He specializes in computer vision and multimodal large models.

His current research focuses on building the next-generation Vision Transformer (ViT) to address urgent needs in modern MLLMs. He is also the #2 contributor to the InsightFace ecosystem (~27k⭐).
